My SP Experience

Life in SP

In my experience, life as an SP student has more freedom than in comparison to that of an ITE or secondary school student. Within SP's 38-hectare campus, there are various facilities that is used for enhancing the learing experience. The food selection is great too with 6 food courts to buy food from, along with outlets such as McDonald's, Starbucks, KFC, and Subway.

School of Computing (SoC)

The School of Computing (SoC) offers 3 full-time diploma courses, that covers Cybersecurity (DISM), AI (DAAA), and App development (DIT) respectively. In my course (DISM), it covers the skills that are required for a career in the cybersecurity field. Currently, in the first semester I have learnt JavaScript programming, computing fundamentals, and front-end web design. In the following semesters I will be learning specialized modules such as ethical hacking and malware reverse engineering.

Summary

In summary, SP campus provides many opportunities for social interactions. That said, the freedom is to be cherished as self-discipline is equally important to be able to complete assignments and homework. On time. Various CCAs and school programs are also available to provide a wholesome education throughout the 3 years journey.
